rulette

about

A rule stacking game based on S7E7 and S8E2 of Game Changer on DropoutTV.

Sign up for Home Edition on Kickstarter.

getting started

  • Whoever names and creates the game will be host.
  • Scan QR code or click shared link to join as player.
  • Nobody can join after the game starts.

rules

  • Players start with 20 points.
  • Spin on your turn to acquire rules.
  • Click to accuse another players of breaking theirs.
  • Host decides how many points to deduct (if any).
